Real Recipe:

Ingredients:
1. Cup of noodles
2. Water
3. Microwave
4. Fork

Procedure:
Step 1: Open the noodle cup 
Step 2: Add water 
Step 3: Microwave for 1 minute 
Step 4: Use a fork to stir
Step 5: Cover the cup / Sit for 2 minutes
Step 6: Stir / Check for firmness 
Step 7: Serve

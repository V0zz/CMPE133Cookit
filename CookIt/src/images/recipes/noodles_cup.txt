Real Recipe:

Ingredients:
1. Cup of noodles
2. Water
3. Microwave
4. Fork/Spoon

Procedure:
Step 1: Open the cup 
Step 2: Add seasoning 
Step 3: Add water 
Step 4: Microwave for 1 minute 
Step 5: Stir
Step 6: Cover the cup / Sit for 2 minutes
Step 7: Stir / Check for firmness 
Step 8: Serve

Real Recipe:

Ingredients:
1. Egg
2. Water
3. Pot
4. Stove

Procedure:
Step 1: Place egg in pot and cover with cold water 
Step 2: Put pot over high heat and bring water to a rolling boil. Remove pan from heat and cover
Step 3: Let the egg stand in the hot water for 14-17 minutes 
Step 4: Drain egg and put in a bowl filled with ice water 
Step 5: Peel the egg

Real Recipe:

Ingredients:
1. 1 egg
2. 1/2 teaspoon salt
3. 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
4. 1 pound ground beef
5. 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
6. Cooking oil

Procedure:
Step 1: Preheat the oven for high heat and lightly oil grate
Step 2: Get a medium bowl 
Step 3: Put egg, salt, and pepper into the bowl 
Step 4: Use a whisk to whisk together these ingredients 
Step 5: Place ground beef and bread crumbs into the mixture
Step 6: Mix until well blended
Step 7: Form into 4 patties approximately 3/4 inch thick 
Step 8: Place patties on the prepared grill
Step 9: Cook 6 to 8 minutes per side

Real Recipe:

Ingredients:
1. Hamburger buns
2. Grilled burger
3. 1-2 slices of cheese
4. Lettuce
5. 2 tomato slices
6. Onion rings
7. Pickles
8. Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Procedure:
Step 1: Cover the hamburger buns with thin layers of ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise
Step 2: Place lettuce on the bottom bun
Step 3: Put tomato slices on top of the lettuce
Step 4: Place the grilled burger after that
Step 5: Add 1-2 slices of cheese on the burger 
Step 6: Place some slices of pickles
Step 7: Put the onion rings onto the pickles
Step 8: Place the top bun on the very top
